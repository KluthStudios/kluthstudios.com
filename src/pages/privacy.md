This Privacy Policy explains how kluthstudios.com (the "Site") handles information. The short version: it barely handles any. This is a static portfolio with no accounts, no forms, no cookies, and no analytics.

**Quick Summary:** The Site does not track you. It sets no cookies, runs no analytics, and builds no profile of your visit. The only personal information we ever receive is what you choose to send by email, and it is never sold or shared for marketing.

1\. Who Runs This Site
----------------------

kluthstudios.com is the portfolio of Kluth Studios. We are responsible for how the limited information described below is handled. You can reach us at hello@kluthstudios.com.

2\. Information the Site Collects
--------------------------------

### Information you choose to send

If you email us using the address on the Site, we receive your email address, your name, and whatever you write. That is entirely your choice, and it is the only way personal information reaches us through this Site.

### Information collected automatically

The Site itself runs no analytics and sets no cookies, so it does not automatically collect anything about you. Like every website, the service that hosts it may keep standard server access logs (such as IP addresses, browser type, and the time of a request) for security and operational reasons. These logs are managed by the hosting provider under its own policies and are not used to identify or profile individual visitors.

The Site does **not** collect account details, passwords, payment information, uploads, or location data, because it has no features that would use them.

3\. Cookies and Tracking
------------------------

The Site uses **no cookies and no tracking technologies**. Fonts and other assets are served directly from the Site rather than from third-party networks, so visiting a page does not hand your visit to an advertiser or analytics company. There are no tracking pixels, no ad networks, and no cross-site profiling.

4\. How Information Is Used
--------------------------

Any email you send is used only to read and reply to your message and, if it leads somewhere, to discuss working together. It is not added to a mailing list, sold, rented, or shared for advertising. Server logs held by the hosting provider are used only to keep the Site running and secure.

5\. Sharing
-----------

Your personal information is never sold. It may be handled by the infrastructure providers that host the Site and deliver our email, acting only on our behalf, and it may be disclosed if genuinely required by law or to protect the safety and rights of others. That is all.

6\. Data Retention
------------------

Emails you send are kept only as long as they are useful for correspondence or an ongoing conversation, and are deleted when they are no longer needed. You can ask us to delete an email exchange at any time. Server access logs are retained according to the hosting provider's standard schedule.

7\. Your Rights
---------------

Because the Site collects so little, exercising your rights is simple. You may ask us to access, correct, or delete any email correspondence you have sent, and we will do so. Depending on where you live, such as the EU, UK, or California, you may have additional statutory rights over personal data; those rights are honored for the limited information described here. To make a request, email hello@kluthstudios.com.

8\. Third-Party Sites
---------------------

The Site links to external sites and services, such as GitHub and project repositories. Once you follow a link, you are on their turf, and their own privacy policies apply. We do not control how those services handle your information.

9\. Children's Privacy
----------------------

The Site is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them. Since the Site collects no information through any form, there is nothing here for a child, or anyone, to submit.

10\. Changes to This Policy
---------------------------

This Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes are posted here with a new "last updated" date.

11\. Contact
------------

Questions about privacy are welcome.

**Email:** hello@kluthstudios.com

_This Policy describes a deliberately minimal site. The best privacy protection is not collecting the data in the first place, which is the approach taken here._
